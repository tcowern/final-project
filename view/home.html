Welcome to the Life Planner "Bucket List" App!

This Applicaton will help you brainstorm ideas for what you want to get accomplished in your life!

  1. First You Will Brainstorm for 5 minutes to add items to your bucket list!
  2. Next you will prioritize your bucket list based on which Bucket List items are most improtant
  3. Step three will be to add conpletion dates to your Bucket List Items
  4. The final step will be to add tasks to each item to give you a firm handle on what it will take to reach your goals!
